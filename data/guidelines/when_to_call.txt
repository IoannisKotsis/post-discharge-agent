# When to call a doctor / red flags

## Call a doctor (NHS)
Source: https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/type-2-diabetes/treatment/

If you feel ill, such as vomiting, diarrhoea or a very high temperature, while you're taking diabetes medicine, speak to a doctor or
a member of your healthcare team. They may tell you to stop taking your medicine temporarily, until you feel better.
Do not stop taking your medicine unless your healthcare professional or doctor tells you to.
